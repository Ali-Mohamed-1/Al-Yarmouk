Cementitious Additives

SAF

Cementing additives to Increase the Setting Time of the cement. They are specialty cementing slurry additive used to increase the thickening time for cement slurries to enable proper placement and is applicable for primary and secondary cementing jobs and is compatible with all API Oil well cements.

AY 20 S

Additives to reduce the setting time and increase the rate of Compressive strength, speeds up or shortens the normal rate of reaction time between cement and water which shortens the thickening time of the cement and increases the early strength of the cement required for the cement slurry to become hardened mass.

Disperse AY-45

Are additives that are pumped between the drilling fluid and the cement slurry to form a buffer and prevent the drilling fluid and the cement slurry from coming into contact.
